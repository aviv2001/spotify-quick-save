Privacy Policy for Spotify Quick Save

Last Updated: November 2023

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Spotify Quick Save ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Spotify authentication tokens (encrypted and stored locally)
  • Selected playlist ID and name
  • Basic playback information about currently playing tracks

How We Use Your Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with Spotify's services
  • Add songs to your selected playlist
  • Display notifications about successful/failed operations

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We use industry-standard encryption for authentication tokens. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension interacts with Spotify's Web API. Your use of Spotify's services is governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Local encryption of authentication tokens
  • Secure authentication using OAuth 2.0 with PKCE
  • Automatic token refresh and secure storage

Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke access through Spotify's account settings
  • Clear local storage by uninstalling the extension
  • View stored playlist information in extension settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at aviv1.work@gmail.com .

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your playlist selection and authentication tokens
  • tabs: To interact with Spotify Web Player tabs
  • activeTab: To add songs from the current playing track
  • identity: For secure authentication with Spotify
  • alarms: For automatic token refresh
  • windows: For handling authentication popups

Data Retention

Local data is retained until you:

  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear browser data
  • Explicitly revoke access
